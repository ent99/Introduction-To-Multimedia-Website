    a collection of some of my favourite hobbies and interests.

    FOOTBALL

    easily my biggest hobby is football. I got into the sport from a young age, playing sunday league from the age of 7, having started watching football before then. I played and trained every week up until around 15 years old, when I started to just play more casually and less frequently.

    MUSIC

    another one of my big hobbies is listening to music. I always have my earphones or headphones on me - I don't leave the house without them. I like to think I listen to a reasonably broad number of genres, my main ones being rap, grime, r and b and soul.

    PHOTOGRAPHY

    whilst not as obvious as my other main hobbies, photography is something that i'm really interested in, and I wish I had more time to actually get involved with. I've held an interest in the area for years, but because of time and money constraints I haven't been able to really get into it, but it remains something I enjoy doing when I can.
